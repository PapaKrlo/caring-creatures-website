Goal # 1: From selling out, donate £100,000 to two charities in the UK

At 25% sellout, we will donate £25,000 to a reputable charity organisation working to address homelessness in the United Kingdom. At 100% sellout we will donate £75,000 to the winning charity of our community election. Both milestones will be reached by selling our profile picture NFT collection.

Goal # 2: Provide sustained support for charities

After our initial donation, we will continue to support different charitable causes by giving them exposure within our community and social media channels. We will also keep contributing financially with funds raised from OpenSea royalties, game nights, digital auctions, and more.

Goal # 3: Create a community you feel proud to belong to

Bring together altruistic individuals that enjoy the idea of constantly supporting charities and improving the world. Allow everyone to have a good time, while also providing a community to express creativity and ideas to tackle real issues.

ABOUT US

What is Caring Creatures?

Caring Creatures is a community started after researching ways of helping the unhoused community in the UK. We aim to bring together passionate individuals wanting to make a positive impact in the United Kingdom. With most of the initial funds raised donated to charities, every NFT purchase helps support those in need.

We allow our holders to decide which charities we'll be supporting across a variety of realms. After successfully donating to our initial cause, we are able to pivot to other charities such as Macmillan Cancer Support, British Heart Foundation, Alzheimer's Society, Children in Need, Dementia UK, and more!

We would be happy to have you in our community, join the project now :)

BID ON UNIQUE ART

Bid on one-of-a-kind digital art pieces made by artists and creatives, all the way up to large NFT collection pieces that are donated, obtained through a collaboration, or purchased at a convenient price.

Holders:

Have a private auction on the art piece through our web app.

Non-holders:

If the reserve price is not met on the holders-only auction, a public auction on OpenSea will take place for holders and non-holders to bid on the art piece.

ONLINE GAME NIGHTS

Connect with fellow Caring Creatures holders in fun virtual events like poker nights and chess tournaments. Compete for exclusive prizes, including Caring Creatures postcards, physical art pieces, and more.

Holders:

Can enjoy playing for free, they don’t have to buy a ticket for our game nights. Voluntary contributions are optional but well-received, as they help us achieve our goals.
There are also special, holders-only game nights.

Non-holders:

Have to buy a ticket through our web app in order to participate in any of game nights.

Unlock endless creative possibilities with full copyright ownership of your Caring Creature. Use it on your short films, illustrations, games, album covers, and more! (T&Cs apply).

Holders:

Get the copyright usage for beneficial personal and commercial purposes.

Non-holders:

No creature, no copyright, no benefits.

NFT RAFFLES

Join the raffles on our web app for exciting prizes. Buy tickets starting from £0.99 and have a chance to win a unique digital collectible. All while you help fund our mission and provide sustained support to charities.

Holders:

For each Caring Creature in their possession, they get an extra raffle ticket. Meaning that if holder “X” has 50 Caring Creatures and they buy 50 raffle tickets, they’ll get 100 tickets total (doubling the chances of winning!). Holders also get a priority time window for entering the raffle.

Non-holders:

Are allowed to buy any number of tickets through the web app after the holders' priority time window. They don’t get any extra tickets with their purchase.

SHAPE THE IMPACT OF CARING CREATURES

When you hold at least one of our NFTs, you become an essential decision-maker for the project. Every six months, we hold democratic elections where our holders decide on the next charity we'll support.

Holders:

Decide on the next charity that gets supported. For each Caring Creature in their possession, they get a vote. Meaning that if holder “Y” has 10 Caring Creatures, they’ll get 10 votes total in our democratic election.

Non-holders:

Don’t get a say in the charities we support.
